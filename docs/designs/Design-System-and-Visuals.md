# Design System and Visual Direction

## Brand Positioning

Luxury, exclusivity, discretion, premium culinary artistry.

---

## Color Palette (Luxury Inspired)

- Deep Black (#0D0D0D)
- Gold Accent (#C6A75E)
- Off White (#F5F5F5)

---

## Typography

Primary Font: Elegant serif (for headlines)
Secondary Font: Clean sans-serif (for body text)

---

## Visual Style

- Minimal layout
- Large typography
- Cinematic spacing
- Dark luxury aesthetic
- High-contrast photography (future phase)

---

## Layout Philosophy

White space = Luxury

No clutter.
No flashy animations.
Structured hierarchy.

---

## Tone of Voice

- Confident
- Exclusive
- Calm
- Premium
- No desperation marketing language
